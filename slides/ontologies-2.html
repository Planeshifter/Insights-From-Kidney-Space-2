Using an Ontology

  • WordNet: Lexical Database developed by George Miller and colleagues at Princeton University over last two decades
  • Unified Medical Language System (UMLS): Vast Dictionary of biomedical and health related concepts (Metathesaurus) plus general ontology of $133$ concepts
  • ConceptNet: Developed at the MIT Media Lab, aggregates information from various sources.

Fellbaum, Christiane (2005). WordNet and wordnets. In: Brown, Keith et al. (eds.), Encyclopedia of Language and Linguistics, Second Edition, Oxford: Elsevier, 665-670
Bodenreider, O. (2004). The Unified Medical Language System (UMLS): integrating biomedical terminology. Nucleic Acids Research, 32.

Language is a challenging problem.

  • The data are unstructured
  • Language is ambiguous
  • Meaning depends often on context
  • Synonymy: Multiple words having the same meaning. Examples:
    • car, automobile
    • affluent, wealthy
  • Polysemy: Same word having different meanings. Examples:
    • bank: river bank, financial institution
    • lemon: fruit, bad car

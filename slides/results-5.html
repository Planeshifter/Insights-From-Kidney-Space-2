Validation

  • First Posts: $50\%$ of opening posts are of people a) just being diagnosed, b) experiencing follow-up disease symptoms or c) advancing to a new stage and/or treatment $[0.398, 0.602]$.
  • Gender: Of $200$ randomly selected users, $94$ members could be identified on the basis of their first post: $67$ were women and $27$ men. $95\%$-CI for proportion of females is $\left[0.61, 0.80\right]$.
  • Treatment: Of all threads mentioning PD and HD, we estimate that $26\%$ ask for a direct comparison between them $[0.17, 0.37]$. Overall, $3\%$ of all posts talking about eiher of the two options ask for such a comparison $[0.018, 0.036]$.
  • Food: Of posts talking about recipes and food, we estimate that $40\% \, [0.28, 0.52]$ contain a recipe request and $36\% \, [0.248, 0.484]$ share a recipe. In total, $3\%$ of posts mentioning a food idem specifically ask for recipes. $[0.026, 0.042]$.

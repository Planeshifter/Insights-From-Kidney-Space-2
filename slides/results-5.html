Validation

  • First Posts: $50\%$ of opening posts are of people a) just being diagnosed, b) experiencing follow-up disease symptoms or c) advancing to a new stage and/or treatment $[39.8, 60.2]$.
  • Gender: Of $200$ randomly selected users, $94$ members could be identified on the basis of their first post: $67$ were women and $27$ men. $95\%$-CI for proportion of females is $\left[61, 80\right]$.
  • Treatment: Of all threads mentioning PD and HD, we estimate that $26\%$ ask for a direct comparison between them $[17, 37]$. Overall, $3\%$ of all posts talking about eiher of the two options ask for such a comparison $[1.8, 3.6]$.
  • Food: Of posts talking about recipes and food, we estimate that $40\% \, [28, 52]$ contain a recipe request and $36\% \, [24.8, 48.4]$ share a recipe. In total, $3\%$ of posts mentioning a food idem specifically ask for recipes. $[2.6, 4.2]$.

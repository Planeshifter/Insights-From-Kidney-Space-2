Synset Relationships: Using WordNet’s Lexical Hierarchy

Contains hierarchy of concepts, ranging from very general ({entity}, {state}) to moderately abstract ({person, individual}) to very specific ({doctor, doc, physician}).

Some terminology: For a given synset, its. . .

  • hypernyms are the synsets that are more general
  • hyponyms are the synsets that are more specific

Hyponyms have an “Is-A" relationship to their hypernyms.

The Landscape

The landscape I am exploring might look familiar, the healthcare sector. Think of hospitals, doctors etc. The Internet, which has emerged as a major source of health-related information, is changing this sector profoundly. Communication about medical questions which has formely faded away now leaves indelible traces. Patients organize themselves in online self-help forums. Looking at the blue icon which looks like a planet system, it represents something we were not able to scrutinize before: communication in social networks.

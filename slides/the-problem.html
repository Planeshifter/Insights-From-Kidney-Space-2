The General Question

Analyzing online self-help forums to shed a light on how chronic diseases affect patient's daily lives, with the goal of extending the view of clinicians.

Scientifically spoken, these forums are still black holes, but hold potential data treasures which can be mined. In the case of chronic diseases online forums, we meet patients whose entire life is affected, and therefore these forums might yield insights into the daily lives of the patients, insights which might help to improve doctor-patient communication.
